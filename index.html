Seasons in Bijapur

The weather seasons

weather seasons

winter

Winter in the city begins in December and continues till February. The average day temperature during the season hovers around 30°C, while the minimum settles close to 16°C. These temperatures are really comfortable for this time of the year. For North Indians, this could be a fantastic place to escape the winter chill and explore the beautiful monuments that dot the city.

summer

The summer season in Vijayapur starts in March and lasts till the end of May. Located in the southern peninsula, the weather during this period is hot with day temperatures touching 43°C at times. On an average the maximum hovers around 38°C. Therefore those eager to explore this place should try and avoid this season.

September with an average rainfall of 150 mm is the rainiest month for the city. The temperatures during the monsoon season drop by a good margin making the weather comfortable. So any plans of travelling to the city can be taken up during this time.
